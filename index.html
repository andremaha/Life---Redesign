Living is easy with eyes closed

I often say to my friends that my life has been perfect sice about 2005. Not mediocre, but perfect - the way that I even think sometimes - Wow, it is just to perfect to be true.

I think I know why it happened - but there is no way to give a one sentence formula. So I decided to take a deductive approache and just show how it is possible.

Working as a designer I get to have an idea, think about it and then form it into the visible object. I believe that applies to everything in this world - you just get the vision and then you make it real - you design your life.

the WHAT

Recent events that have no meaning for the reader, but are meaningful to me.

the WHY

Because of the entropy. After all it is the only was to keep the track of all the WHAT.

the HOW
